ABOUT


Workshop PoV is a banner for Design, Architecture, Film making and Allied fields based in New Delhi.
With a young team of dedicated, enthusiastic and discerning professionals with diverse work experiences and interests.
With more than 8 years of working experience in the field of Architecture design and intervention as well as Film Making and Script Writing, we have developed a good network of professionals to provide you with a complete solution.
Our emphasis on making things that are functional and yet have aesthetic value by virtue of their simplicity. Each project is rendered unique and goes through its own processes.

FOUNDING PARTNERS

Aditya Mazumdar, the fellow principle architect at Workshop PoV, completed his diploma in architecture in 2008, allowing him his first foray into the world of architecture, when he worked as an assistant architect at a boutique architecture firm. Even as he was learning on the job, he completed his B.Arch Degree from Aayojan School of Architecture, Jaipur (affiliated to YCMOU) in 2015. Furthermore, he received an apprenticeship in Earth Construction from Ladhak, India, in 2013, and did a Master's Program from the Dessau Institute of Architecture, Germany in 2016. His professional repertoire includes various projects of scale ranging from exhibition design to interior design, residntial architecture to institutional and hospital projects, not to mention numerous Master-plan proposals.

Shamsher Singh is an architect who completed his B.Arch course from MEASI Academy of Architecture, Chennai. Since graduating in 2009-10, he practiced as an assistant architect under various architects of repute in Delhi. In Workshop PoV, he serves as one of the two Principle architects. As a young Architect, He has worked at various scales, including exhibition design, interior design, residential architecture, and institutional architecture and Master planning. Being a process oriented person, he holds strong the belief that rather than dictate terms, architecture, design and built form should create opportunities for the future while being a commentary on the present.

Anurag Sikder has been academically trained in economics and business. Under the aegis of online media and film making organisations, he trained to be a writer and a multi media storyteller. In Workshop PoV, he serves as the lead writer and producer of literary and cinematic productions. His works include short stories, fiction films and documentaries. It is routine to find an impression of his personal beliefs about sustainability and transparency in his creations.
