ABOUT


Workshop PoV is a banner for Design, Architecture , Film making and Allied fields based in New Delhi.
With a young team of dedicated, enthusiastic and discerning professionals with diverse work experiences and interests.
With more than 8 years of working experience in the field of Architecture design and intervention as well as Film Making and Script Writing, we have developed a good network of professionals to provide you with a complete.
