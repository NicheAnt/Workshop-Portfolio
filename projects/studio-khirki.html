STUDIO KHIRKI
AREA : 8000 Sq.Ft (over 4 floors) to be expanded to 10,000 Sq.Ft by adding a 5th floor.
LOCATION :Khirki Village , Delhi
YEAR :2018
STATUS :On-Going
SERVICES PROVIDED :Conceptual Design | Interior Design | Rennovation | Lighting Design | Site Supervision
TAGS: Interiors | Pinboard

Previously a Leather Design House and Production unit. This 20 odd year old building in the bustling urban village of Khirki , was to take on a new avatar- as a studio/gallery and performance space. We were brought on board to help the clients plan and visualise this. Alongside - the client also wanted to host TIRAF (the irregulars art fair). So we came on as design partners for the event. Converting the the basement, ground and First levels into a gallery and display space for over 30 shortlisted artists. The three day event was hosted sucessfuly , and now the space continues to host and cater to new events.
