STUDIO KHIRKI
AREA : 10,000 sqft (over 5 floors)
LOCATION :Khirki Village , Delhi
YEAR :2018
STATUS :On-Going
SERVICES PROVIDED :Conceptual Design | Interior Design | Rennovation | Lighting Design | Site Supervision
TAGS: Interiors | Exhibition

Previously a Leather Design House and Production unit. This 20 odd year old building in the bustling urban village of Khirki , was to take on a new avatar- as a studio/gallery and performance space. We were brought on board to help the clients plan and visualise this. Alongside - the client also wanted to host TIRAF (the irregulars art fair , link - http://www.theirregularsartfair.com/). So we came on as design partners for the event. Converting the the basement, ground and First levels into a gallery and display space for over 30 shortlisted artists. The three day event was hosted sucessfuly , and now we're are back to the drawing board - towards a more permanent solution for the space.
