A penthouse in a hi-end apartment complex in Gurgaon was designed for a joint family of seven as a transit house. A six-bedroom penthouse was designed with formal living and family living spaces, a TV lounge area, a large kitchen & utility. All bedrooms have attached balconies landscaped in stone and low-maintenance evergreen plantations.The bifurcation of the public and private spaces gave privacy when required and the common spaces around thefireplace as well as common amenities provided the intimacy of a family home. With minimalist details, careful use of material & color palette, the prime motive was achieved, which was to design a low maintenance but a highly functional second home.
