TAGS: Interiors





Interiors for a living room in a Vasant Vihar Residence. The client required and upheaval of the existing design- a space where they could work and entertain

The Tv unit was made with lacquered glass and cement board shutters. The lacquered glass was to hide the TV when it was off, and the cement board panels – to disguise what was infact a storage unit. Removing the wooden finishing from the doors and windows –and instead adding one focus point- the parametric ribbed wall . What was achieved was a living and dining that could function as spaces for entertainment as well as business meetings, while keeping the quality of spaciousness intact. We had also beeen comissioned to re-design the terrace area into space that could be used for functions and parties. The terrace was to include a Servants quarters and Utility as well - which we screened off from vision using a Parametric brick wall. Along with this a new staircase to the Mumpty and Powder room were also suggested.
