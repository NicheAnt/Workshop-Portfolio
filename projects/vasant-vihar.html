VASANT VIHAR RESIDENCE
AREA: Total Carpet area of 800 sq. ft (only Living room)
LOCATION: New Delhi, IN
YEAR: 2016
STATUS: Completed
SERVICES PROVIDED: Interior Design | 3D Visualizations | Costing and BOQ | MEP Services and Consultation | Working/Construction/Shop Drawings | Site Co-ordination and Project Management
TAGS: Interiors | Residential





Interiors for a living room in a Vasant Vihar Residence. The client required an upheaval of the existing space where they could work as well as entertain.

Black lacquered glass and cement board shutters were used for TV unit. The lacquered glass camouflaged with the TV when off and the cement board panels disguised the storage unit. Wooden finishing from the doors and windows was removed and a parametric ribbed wall was added as the only focus point. A living and dining that could function as spaces for entertainment as well as business meetings was achieved as a result, while keeping the spatial quality intact.

We were also commissioned to design the terrace for gatherings, with utility and two servants’ room. A brick parametric wall in white was designed to screen the utility and servants’ room on the terrace; however, it was put on hold.
