LOCATION : New Delhi, India
YEAR : 2016
STATUS : Completed
SERVICES PROVIDED : Interior Design | 3D Visualizations | Costing and BOQ | MEP Services and Consultation | Working/Construction/Shop Drawings | Site Co-ordination and Project Management | Graphics and Signage
TAGS: Interiors





Interiors for a living room in a Vasant Vihar Residence. The client required and upheaval of the existing design- a space where they could work and entertain

The Tv unit was made with lacquered glass and cement board shutters. The lacquered glass was to hide the TV when it was off, and the cement board panels – to disguise what was infact a storage unit. Removing the wooden finishing from the doors and windows –and instead adding one focus point- the parametric ribbed wall . What was achieved was a living and dining that could function as spaces for entertainment as well as business meetings, while keeping the quality of spaciousness intact.
