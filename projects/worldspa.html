This project was a high-end two-floor penthouse in Gurugram, for a family of four. Formerly a six-bedroom penthouse, this house was efficiently remodeled into five bedrooms, formal living and family living spaces, a library/study area achieved by converting one bedroom at lower floor, a larger kitchen & utility, and also incorporated gym and sauna on the upper floor. Bedrooms and living spaces spilled to terraces & balconies, enhancing the outdoor living. The finished product was an outcome of expression envisaged in Marble, Stone and Wood and maintaining efficient and minimalist details.
