VASANT KUNJ RESIDENCE
AREA : Total Carpet area of 1700 Sq.ft. over 2 floors
LOCATION : Vasant Kunj, New Delhi, India
YEAR : 2016-2017
STATUS : Completed
SERVICES PROVIDED : Interior Design | 3D Visualizations | Costing | Working/Construction/Shop Drawings | Site Co-ordination | Addition and Extension Design
TAGS: Interior, Residential

A family of two had recently become a family of three, and needed more space in their house. A young clientele that allowed us to keep things minimal and Boho.

The original 950 sq.ft apartment underwent additional work to increase the carpet area to 1700 sqft by adding a floor that was connected by a hardwood spiral staircase. The addition was done using mainly steel as the structural element and pre-cast concrete panels for the infill. The roofs were sloping, and in PUFF panels. The dining room was double height, and was converted from an earlier terrace.

The interiors were kept minimal and bohemian, with a strong emphasis on wood.
